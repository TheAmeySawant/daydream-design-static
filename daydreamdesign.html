About the Founder

Vikrant Vennam, The founder of Daydream Design began his design journey in 2012, completing his interior design studies at Kala Vidya Sankul. After graduation, he gained invaluable experience working with renowned companies like Talati & Panthaky for five years and Space Dynamix for four years.

Seeking broader opportunities, he moved to Dubai, where he worked with SDA Design for two years. This experience allowed him to engage in a diverse range of high-profile projects, broadening his design perspective and providing him with a global outlook on the industry.

In 2021, he founded Daydream Design with a vision of providing personalized, creative, and high-quality design services, helping clients bring their dream spaces to life.
